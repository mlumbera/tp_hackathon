Quick Indiana Chicken & Broccoli

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Broccoli
  • Olive oil
  • Salt (use reduced)
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Cut the chicken breast into bite-size pieces.
  2. Steam broccoli until tender.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and cook chicken until golden and cooked through.
  4. Add broccoli, salt, and pepper. Stir everything together for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy your healthy quick meal!
